Paybuddy is an AI powered finacial insight application, that gives you insight into your transactions.

Our API uses HTTP response codes and returns all error responses in JSON. To explore the API as much as possible,
we offer a developer environment in addition to production.
Our API gives developers the ability to build products ontop your platform. You can easily perfom the following operations:

1. Link bank account to your bank
2. Get all your transaction datas
3. Get Insight into your account through account query and account summaries
4. Categorize your transactions
5. Create budgets and see insights into your transactions
6. Create Expense and Income tracking
7. Real time transaction tracking
